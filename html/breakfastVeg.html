Blueberry Ice Cream

Blueberry Ice Cream is a luscious and fruity treat that combines the sweetness of blueberries with the creaminess of homemade ice cream. Enjoy the burst of flavor in every spoonful.

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Serving Bowls
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation steps

  1. Blend Blueberries: Puree the blueberries in a blender or food processor until smooth.
  2. Combine Ingredients: In a bowl, mix blueberry puree, milk, heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Freeze: Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Feel free to adjust sugar quantities based on your sweetness preference and enjoy these homemade ice creams as refreshing desserts.

Oat Porridge

a comforting oat porridge that offers a warm and nourishing start to your day. Made with simple ingredients, it's easy to prepare and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Pinch of salt

Preparation steps

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a saucepan, combine the rolled oats, milk, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Cook on Stove: Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Serve: Once the oats are soft and the consistency is to your liking, remove from heat and serve warm. You can top it with fruits, nuts, or sweeteners if desired.

Hummus Without Tahini

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation steps

  1. Blend Ingredients: Combine chickpeas, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, and pepper in a food processor.
  2. Blend Until Smooth: Process until a smooth paste forms. Adjust consistency with water if needed.
  3. Serve: Drizzle with olive oil and enjoy with pita, veggies, or crackers.

Crunchy Buckwheat Granola

a crunchy and wholesome blend of oats, buckwheat, and nuts. Sweetened with honey or maple syrup, this granola adds a delightful crunch to your breakfast or snacks.

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup buckwheat groats
  • 1/2 cup nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts), chopped
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Heat the oven, mix oats, buckwheat, nuts, honey, melted coconut oil, vanilla, and salt.
  2. Mix Ingredients: Spread on a baking sheet and bake, stirring for even browning.
  3. Cool and Store: Allow to cool before storing in an airtight container.

Oats with Skyr

Oats with Skyr offers a creamy and protein-packed twist on traditional oatmeal. By combining rolled oats with Icelandic skyr and your favorite fruits, you create a delicious and satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup skyr (Icelandic yogurt)
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Fresh fruits or berries for topping

Preparation steps

  1. Combine Oats and Liquid: Mix rolled oats with skyr and milk.
  2. Soak Overnight: Cover and refrigerate overnight for a creamy texture.
  3. Top and Serve: Before serving, add fresh fruits or berries as toppings. Stir and enjoy!
