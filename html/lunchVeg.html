Lunch is an important meal for everyone. It provides energy and nutrients to keep the body and brain working efficiently through the afternoon.
A packed lunch made at home can be a healthy and delicious choice and gives you control over the foods and ingredients included.

Mint Icecream

Fruity overnight oats with persimmons and carrots, hidden under a crunchy chocolate layer

Ingredients for 2 servings

  • 80 g fine oat flakes
  • 2 tbsp linseed
  • 300 ml oat drink (oat milk)
  • salt
  • Â½ tsp turmeric
  • Â½ tsp cinnamon
  • persimmons
  • 100 g carrots
  • 20 g hazelnuts
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Preparation steps

  1. Mix the oat flakes together with linseed, oat drink and salt, turmeric and cinnamon. Cut the persimmon into small cubes, finely grate the carrot and mix both into the mixture. Then place in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  2. After the waiting time, divide into two glasses, roughly chop the hazelnuts and fold in â…” of the kernels. Put coconut oil in a pot and let it melt over a low heat for 3-5 minutes and stir with cocoa powder until smooth.
  3. Smooth out the oatmeal and persimmon mixture and pour the coconut oil mixture over it. Swirl the glasses slightly so that a smooth surface forms. As soon as the chocolate layer has set, serve the chocolate shell oats with the remaining hazelnuts.
